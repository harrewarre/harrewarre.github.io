Cloud Native Chronicles - Product Mindset

A new episode launched on the Cloud Native Chronicles podcast and we’re talking about Product Mindset. We’re a bit all over the place with this one but I think the main message is clear: Think about what you want to build and for who. In this episode of Cloud Native Chronicles, hosts Johan and Sander explore the concept of a product mindset in software development. They discuss the differences between building software and creating a product, emphasizing the importance of understanding stakeholder needs and engaging with them throughout the development process.
