The great quarantine of 2020

We’re almost at the end of 2020 and we’re not really quarantining anymore but kinda still are. From-home all the things The biggest game changer in this whole pandemic: companies are forced to figure out how to let folks work from home. I was working from home 2 days a week already so the change wasn’t that extreme but 5 days, especially during the full lockdown we had with the whole family together was someting entirely new.
