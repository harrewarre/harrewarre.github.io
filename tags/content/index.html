Linkstream

I read a lot of stuff on the internet and after reading about linkblogs I figured “why not?”.

So as of today, there’s a new section on my site where you can follow along with the stuff I find noteworthy. They won’t be full posts but short snippets, quotes and of course, the link to the article.

I do want to keep the links seperate from the rest of the content just to give the option of not having to see the links.
