Read comics on your Kindle e-reader

Vacation is coming and I was looking at stuff to put on my Kindle e-reader. Found some books to read and then I came across comic books on Amazon. Nothing sparked my interest right away but it did remind me of some old comics I read a looooong time ago (of which I still have the actual paper versions!). The specific comics are about Storm, a dude’s adventure after falling into Jupiters great red spot and accidentally traveling through time in the process.
