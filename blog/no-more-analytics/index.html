No more analytics

I've decided to remove all analytics tracking from my website. The data has no meaningful purpose for me (I'm not pulling in any crazy numbers of traffic to begin with) and the writing is something I do when I feel like it.

I was using fairanalytics.de for their privacy-first stance on how analytics should be done. Highly recommended you check them out if you are looking for a privacy friendly analytics solution.
